关于 LUER

LUER（鹿了）是一款 iOS 签到类应用，以「鹿」为主题，帮助您记录每日状态并与鹿友互动。更多权利义务说明见《用户协议》与《隐私政策》。

功能使用说明

1. 登录与首次使用

在登录页使用「通过 Apple 登录」。可打开保持登录；在凭证有效期间再次打开应用会保留会话，过期后需重新通过 Apple 登录。新用户在符合规则时会自动建号，并可能需先阅读并同意《用户协议》《隐私政策》后完成注册。首次使用若尚未设置显示昵称，会进入设置昵称，保存后在鹿友等处以该名称展示（昵称有长度与内容规则，且不可含违规内容）。

若同一 Apple ID 历史上已绑定多个 LUER 账号，登录时可能需从中选择一个进入；新注册时同一 Apple ID 在云端仅可再新增一个账号。忘记用户名或密码时，可在（登录页）设置 → 账号 → 忘记账号或密码，用 Apple 验证后查看该 Apple ID 下全部绑定账号的账号名与系统生成的密码，用于在设置里修改密码等操作。

2. 主界面与底部栏

登录后主界面为签到、鹿友、动态三栏（底部玻璃胶囊条，从左到右）。点选名称或左右滑动中间指示条即可切换。首次进入「鹿友」或「动态」时，如尚未同意对应条款，会弹出全屏或居中说明，同意且网络可用后方可进入。鹿友、动态与签到之间靠底栏来回切换，无单独系统级「返回」栈。

3. 签到与月历

  • 含义：在可签到的日期上选择 没鹿（记为 Yes，日历为红色格）或 鹿了（记为 No，绿色格），表示该日状态。与连续天数、顶部 Day N 等计算规则以应用内展示为准。
  • 已签过的日期：不能在同一天再次改 Yes/No，但可点击该日打开日期备忘录，记录多行文字（有字数上限与保存/不保存/取消）。
  • 补签：仅可对「自昨天起往前 4 天内、尚未签」的日期补签。这类日期在月历上常为黄色；月历下方也可通过「昨天鹿了吗？」以 Yes/No 补记昨天。若有多天待补，会按时间顺序逐日弹窗选择。
  • 忍耐力：与连续签没鹿/鹿了相关，点顶栏 忍耐力 可查看说明与变动历史（如签到、精力兑换、兑换背景、昨日签到模版动态奖励等来源）。
  • 月历操作：左右箭头换月，「今日」回当前月。在月历区域长按可复制自己的 6 位 UID。月历卡右下角有分享，可生成方形分享预览图并保存到相册（会请求相册权限）。

4. 顶栏快捷

  • 左上竖列（上/下顺序以界面为准）：购物袋 — 打开内购，购买精力（需能连上服务器时方可打开）。信封 — 官方邮件收件箱，查看广播通知；若含忍耐力/精力等需在邮件详情内领取。邮件可有过期时间、按版本显示等，以实际列表与详情为准。可与「无法连接」类提示分开展示的文案对应。
  • 右上竖列：齿轮 — 打开 设置。奖杯 — 打开 排行榜（见第 8 节）。

5. 鹿友

  • 在「寻找鹿友」中输入对方 6 位 UID 搜索，找到后可添加为好友。可使用扫一扫或从相册识别带 LUER_DEER: 前缀的鹿友二维码；「我的鹿友码」可生成带二维码的分享图并保存到相册。
  • 待处理的好友申请显示在相关页面上方，可同意或拒绝。加好友有数量上限，不能添加自己或重复添加。
  • 在列表中可展开鹿友卡片：查看记录（若对方未隐藏）看连续/累计等统计；点赞（每鹿友每天限一次）；修改备注；举报；以及删除并屏蔽等（以界面按钮为准）。
  • 在「隐私设置」中可开启隐藏我的记录（他人查看你记录时见隐藏提示）、隐藏我的动态（他人在动态中看不到你发的帖，本人仍可见自己）、屏蔽新好友申请等。屏蔽关系会影响动态可见与添加，详见《用户协议》。

6. 称号与背景

在鹿友页个人卡片上点击称号区域，进入称号选择页。上方可在「选称号 / 选背景」间切换。未解锁称号带锁，已解锁的显示获得日期；部分称号会登记全服第几位编号。付费主题背景（如金色/龙/鹿意满盈等）需用忍耐力购买后永久解锁；忍耐力不足时可按提示用精力按 1:1 兑换。选好右上角确认保存。

7. 动态

  • 时间线可切换 推荐 与 我的鹿友 等分段，展示纯文字动态；下拉可刷新。动态按发布时间倒序，本人动态始终会出现在自己的列表中（若对方在隐私里对你隐藏动态，则规则以应用与数据为准）。
  • 点右上角进入发布：正文有字节/违规校验。若昨天已在签到里记过 Yes/No，可选使用昨日签到模版（可配正文颜色）；该方式成功发帖可额外获得 +2 忍耐力，且每个 UTC 自然日仅能通过模版成功一次。也可改回自由输入发其他内容。
  • 互动：可点赞。点评论在下方直接评论；短按某条评论进入回复（楼中楼）；长按评论可出现复制/举报/删除等。他人动态右上角 ⋯ 可举报等；仅可删除自己发的动态。推荐流中作者 UID 可轻点复制。

8. 排行榜

在签到页点顶栏奖杯打开。内分周榜、月榜、称号等：周/月榜可用左右箭头换不同 ISO 周或不同自然月（不能选尚未开始的未来周/月）。在周期榜中可选全服 / 鹿友、选择统计指标（如连续/累计等）与排序方向。若你开启了「隐藏我的记录」，可能从相应排行统计中排除。称号页展示配置中可参与全服排行的称号等。

9. 设置

从签到页或登录页点齿轮进入。栏目顺序以界面为准，一般包含：

  • 通用：显示（浅色/深色/跟随系统）、语言（简中/繁中/英/日）、提醒（每日签到提醒开关与时间）。
  • 工具 — 图片修改：格式转换、像素修改、图片压缩、制作长图（多图纵向拼接，最多约 50 张分批添加，保存到相册）。文件编辑 — 扫描文件：用相机拍多页文档，合并为 PDF 后通过系统界面保存到「文件」、iCloud 等；PDF 调整：导入多份 PDF 合并、整理页面顺序等，导出到自选位置。以上均在本机处理，默认不经过 LUER 服务器上传。
  • 账号（签到后）：修改昵称、修改密码、注销账号。登录页设置另有忘记账号或密码，见第 1 节。
  • 记录：展示你本人的最高连续、累计、最高忍耐力等统计（有数据时显示）。
  • 帮助与支持：本页技术支持、隐私政策、用户协议。
  • 退出账号：仅登出，不删除云端数据。

10. 精力、内购与兑换

点顶栏购物袋购买精力（以页面标价与 App Store/StoreKit 为准，需能连接服务器）。精力可在忍耐力相关场景按 1:1 换为忍耐力。购买、兑换依赖网络，失败时以系统或应用内提示为准。

11. 主屏幕小组件

在 iOS 主屏幕可添加 LUER 小组件（小、中两种尺寸），展示月历与昨天签到等摘要；语言与主应用一致。中尺寸在规则允许时可通过桌面 Yes/No 为「昨天」补记（会校验网络，失败则不写入，避免与云端不一致）。

常见问题（FAQ）

如何通过 Apple 登录与账号限制？

您可使用「通过 Apple 登录」注册或登录。每个 Apple ID 仅可绑定 1 个 LUER 账号（新建账号时受此限制；历史若存在多账号，登录流程以应用内实际提示为准）。若您使用用户名与密码，账号为 6–20 位，仅支持数字、英文字母与下划线；密码至少 8 位，仅支持数字与英文字母。

如何添加鹿友？

在签到页进入「鹿友」→「寻找鹿友」，输入对方 6 位 UID 搜索并发送申请。对方同意后即可成为鹿友。长按日历区域可复制自己的 UID 分享给他人。

补签有什么限制？

仅限自昨天起往前 4 天内、尚未签到的日期可补签。点击日历上高亮可补签日期，或使用「昨天鹿了吗？」等入口进行补签（以应用内实际展示为准）。

忍耐力是什么？

忍耐力在连续签「没鹿」（No）时累积，每次签 No 增加一定值，签「鹿了」（Yes）不增加。点击顶部「忍耐力」可查看说明与历史。

称号如何解锁？

不同称号条件不同，例如连续多日签 Yes、距上次签 No 的间隔等。在鹿友页个人信息卡片的称号区域可查看与切换已解锁称号（具体以应用内与云端配置为准）。

如何修改密码或注销账号？

打开设置 → 账号，可修改密码或发起注销。注销需按流程二次确认并验证身份；完成后数据将按《隐私政策》与产品说明处理并返回未登录状态。

鹿友数量有上限吗？

每人最多可添加 50 个鹿友。

如何举报违规内容？会如何处理？

在「鹿友」中可对违规昵称举报；在「动态」中可对单条动态通过「⋯」选择「举报」。请勿恶意举报。核实后，我们将依规则处理；对动态举报成立的，可删除该条动态，具体以《用户协议》及平台规则为准。

针对动态的举报，后台按每一条被举报的动态单独成条（一条动态一条记录），不与昵称举报混同。单条动态举报记录中至少会包含下表信息，便于核对与处置：

动态标识 该条动态在系统中的唯一 ID（momentId），用于精确定位要审核或删除的那一条
正文信息 含 momentId、momentText（正文全文；旧数据可能为 momentTextPreview）
被举报用户 发布该动态的用户账号、昵称及 UID
举报信息 举报类型、举报时间、举报人账号等

故障排查

无法登录或注册失败

当前登录页以通过 Apple 登录为主。请检查：

  • 网络是否正常（拉取账号列表、自动注册等需联网）
  • Apple ID授权是否完成、是否被系统或家长控制限制
  • 保持登录过期后需重新通过 Apple 登录（非账号密码错误类提示）
  • 若走「忘记账号或密码」查看系统生成的密码后，在设置 → 修改密码时，新密码须符合字符与长度规则

若仍无法解决，可切换网络或稍后再试，并将设备型号、系统版本、出现提示原文与操作步骤发至文末邮箱。

签到或鹿友数据未同步

请检查网络是否正常、是否在多设备同时使用同一账号（可能产生数据冲突）。建议在网络稳定时重新进入应用，数据将按服务逻辑同步。

签到提醒不工作

请检查：应用内设置是否已开启提醒；系统「设置 → 通知 → LUER」是否允许通知；是否开启勿扰、低电量模式等（可能影响推送）。

添加鹿友失败

可能原因：UID 输入错误（须为 6 位）、对方已是鹿友或已发送过申请、您或对方的鹿友数量已达上限、不能添加自己为鹿友等。

应用闪退或异常

可尝试从多任务中完全关闭应用后重试；将 iOS 更新至较新版本。若问题持续，请发邮件并说明设备型号、系统版本、复现步骤与（如有）崩溃大致时间。

About LUER

LUER is an iOS check-in app built around a deer (“鹿”) theme. It helps you log daily status and connect with friends in the app. For legal terms, see the User Agreement and Privacy Policy.

How to use the app

1. Sign-in and first launch

Use Sign in with Apple on the login screen. Turn on Stay signed in if you want the app to restore your session until the credential expires; after that, sign in with Apple again. New users may see the User Agreement and Privacy Policy and must accept them before an account is created. If a display name is required, you will set it in Set display name first; the name is shown to friends and must follow length and content rules (forbidden words are blocked).

If your Apple ID has more than one legacy LUER account, you may need to pick one to enter. New registrations are limited to one new account per Apple ID on the server. To see usernames and auto-generated passwords for all accounts on this Apple ID, use (on the login screen) Settings → Account → Forgot username or password after Apple verification—useful when changing your password in Settings.

2. Main tabs

After sign-in, the app has three main tabs in the bottom bar (left to right): Check-in, Friends, and Dynamics. Tap a label or drag the pill indicator. The first time you open Friends or Dynamics you may need to accept their terms and have a network connection. There is no separate back stack between these—use the bottom bar to switch.

3. Check-in and calendar

  • Types: On a day you can still check in, choose “没鹿” (stored as Yes, red cell) or “鹿了” (stored as No, green cell). Streaks and the top Day N follow the rules shown in the app.
  • Already signed days: You cannot change Yes/No the same day, but you can tap the day to add or edit a note (length limit; save / discard / cancel).
  • Make-up: You can only fill missed days in a rolling window (from yesterday back several days) if they are still unsigned. Those days are often yellow on the calendar. Use the “yesterday” row below the calendar to log yesterday with Yes/No. Multiple missed days are handled one by one in order.
  • Patience (忍耐力): Shown in the top bar; open it for details and history (check-ins, energy exchange, background purchases, yesterday template rewards, etc.).
  • Calendar: Arrows change month; “Today” returns to the current month. Long-press the calendar area to copy your 6-digit UID. Share (near UID) exports a square preview image; you can save it to the Photos library (permission required).

4. Top bar shortcuts

  • Left column (order as in the app): Shopping bag — in-app purchase for energy (requires a working server connection to open). Envelopes — official mail inbox; read notices and, if included, claim endurance/energy in the message detail. Mail may expire or be targeted by app version—follow what you see in the list and detail.
  • Right column: Gear — Settings. Trophy — Leaderboard (see section 8 below).

5. Friends

  • Under Find friend, search by 6-digit UID, then Add. You can Scan a QR or pick from Photos (payload prefix LUER_DEER:). My friend code opens a full-screen code image you can save.
  • Pending friend requests appear at the top; Accept or Decline. You cannot add yourself or exceed the friend cap.
  • Expand a friend’s card: View stats (unless they hide), like (once per friend per day), edit note, report, or remove & block as shown.
  • Privacy: Options include hide my stats from others’ “view record”, hide my moments from others’ feed (you still see your own), and block new friend requests. Blocking affects dynamics visibility and add rules—see the User Agreement.

6. Titles and backgrounds

On the Friends page, tap your title on the profile card to open Title selection. Use the segment to switch between title and background. Locked titles are marked; unlocked ones may show the unlock date. Some titles show a global “No. X” rank. Paid backgrounds cost patience (with optional energy exchange 1:1 if you are short). Tap Confirm to save.

7. Dynamics (feed)

  • Switch segments such as Recommended and My friends’ for text posts. Pull to refresh. Your own posts always appear in your own feed. Others’ visibility may depend on their privacy and blocking rules.
  • Tap the compose entry to post (byte limits and word filters apply). If you checked in yesterday, you can use the yesterday check-in template and optional text color for that template; a successful template post grants +2 patience, once per UTC day for that method. You can also switch to free text for other posts.
  • Interactions: Like. Comment to reply to the post; short-press a comment to reply in thread; long-press a comment for copy / report / delete (as permitted). ⋯ on others’ posts for Report. You can delete only your own posts. Tap a UID chip to copy the author’s UID in recommended views.

8. Leaderboard

Open from the trophy on the check-in top bar. Sections include weekly, monthly, and title rankings. For period boards, use arrows to change ISO week or calendar month (future weeks/months that have not “started” yet are disabled). Choose global vs. friends, the metric, and sort order. If you turn on hide my stats, you may be excluded from relevant rankings. The title tab lists configured title races and unlock counts.

9. Settings

Tap the gear from the check-in or login page. Sections typically include:

  • General: Appearance (light / dark / system), Language (Simplified/Traditional Chinese, English, Japanese), Reminder (daily reminder switch and time).
  • Tools — Image: Format conversion, Resize (pixels), Compress, Long image (vertical stitch, many photos in batches, save to Photos). Files — Document scan (multi-page to PDF, export via the system Files picker), PDF tools (merge and reorder pages across files, export to a path you pick). All run on-device by default—nothing is uploaded to LUER for these tools.
  • Account (after sign-in): Change display name, Change password, Delete account. The login page also has Forgot username or password (see section 1 above).
  • Stats / Records: your own bests (when data exists).
  • Help & support: this technical support page, Privacy Policy, and User Agreement.
  • Sign out: leaves data on the server; you will need to sign in again.

10. Energy, IAP, and exchange

Open the shopping bag to buy energy (price per App Store / StoreKit; requires server reachability). Energy can be exchanged for patience 1:1 where the app offers it. If purchase or exchange fails, follow the on-screen message.

11. Home screen widget

Add the LUER widget (small and medium), showing calendar and “yesterday” check-in summary; language matches the app. The medium layout may let you tap Yes/No for “yesterday” on the Home Screen when the rules allow, with a network check—on failure, nothing is written, to stay consistent with the cloud.

FAQ

Sign in with Apple and account limits

Use Sign in with Apple to register or sign in. Each Apple ID may be linked to one LUER account only for new sign-ups (if you have legacy accounts, follow the in-app flow). If you use a username and password: username 6–20 characters, letters, digits, underscore; password at least 8 characters, letters and digits only.

How to add friends

Open Friends, then Find friend, enter the other person’s 6-digit UID, and send a request. After they accept, you become friends. Long-press the calendar area to copy your own UID to share.

Make-up check-in window

You can only make up missed days within a limited window (from yesterday back several days) for days that have no check-in yet. Use the calendar highlights or the “yesterday” entry points shown in the app (UI may vary by version).

What is “patience” (忍耐力)?

It builds up when you check in with “No” on consecutive days; each “No” adds to it; “Yes” does not. Tap the patience indicator at the top for details and history.

How titles (badges) unlock

Each title has its own conditions (e.g. consecutive “Yes” days, time since last “No”, etc.). View and switch unlocked titles on the profile card in Friends (rules follow in-app and server config).

Change password or delete account

Go to Settings → Account to change your password or start account deletion. Deletion requires confirmation and verification; data handling follows the Privacy Policy and in-app text.

Friend list limit

You can add up to 50 friends.

Reporting violations

Report inappropriate nicknames from Friends. For a post in Dynamics, use “⋯” on that post and choose Report. Do not abuse reports. After review, we may take action; if a dynamics report is upheld, that post may be removed, per the User Agreement and platform rules.

Each dynamics report is tracked per post (one record per reported post), separate from nickname reports. Each record includes at least:

Post ID Unique momentId for the exact post to review or remove
Text Includes momentId, momentText (full text; older data may use momentTextPreview)
Reported user Account, display name, and UID of the author
Report metadata Type, time, reporter account, etc.

Troubleshooting

Cannot sign in or register

Sign-in is primarily Sign in with Apple. Check:

  • Network (loading users and auto-registration need connectivity)
  • Apple authorization completed; Screen Time / restrictions
  • After Stay signed in expires, sign in with Apple again (there is no classic “wrong password” field on the login screen)
  • If you change password in Settings after viewing the generated password under Forgot username or password, the new password must meet the character rules

Try another network or wait and retry. If it persists, email us your device model, iOS version, exact message text, and steps.

Check-in or friend data out of sync

Check your connection and whether the same account is used on several devices (which can cause conflicts). Reopen the app on a stable network; data will sync per server rules.

Reminders not firing

Enable reminders in the app Settings; allow notifications for LUER under iOS Settings → Notifications; check Focus / Low Power Mode and similar settings that may delay alerts.

Cannot add a friend

Common causes: wrong 6-digit UID, request already sent or already friends, you or the other user hit the 50-friend cap, or you cannot add yourself.

Crash or odd behavior

Force-quit and relaunch; update iOS. If it continues, email with model, iOS version, steps to reproduce, and approximate time of the issue.
