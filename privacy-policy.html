LUER（鹿了）隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 3 月

一、引言

LUER（鹿了）是一款基于 iOS 的签到应用，以「鹿」为主题，帮助用户记录每日状态并与鹿友互动。我们重视您的隐私，本隐私政策说明我们如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的个人信息。

二、我们收集的信息

为提供应用功能，我们可能收集以下信息：

  • 账户信息：账号（用户名）、密码、昵称（显示名）、6 位 UID
  • 签到数据：每日签到记录（Yes/No）、连续签到天数、忍耐力、精力值
  • 社交数据：鹿友列表、好友申请、点赞记录
  • 个人内容：日期笔记（您自愿填写的备注）
  • 设备与偏好：主题设置、语言偏好、提醒时间、登录状态（本地存储）

三、信息的使用

我们使用上述信息用于：

  • 创建和管理您的账户
  • 提供签到、鹿友、称号、点赞等功能
  • 存储您的笔记与偏好设置
  • 处理应用内购买（精力等）
  • 发送本地签到提醒（若您开启）

四、数据存储与第三方服务

您的数据存储在受信任的云服务商：

  • Firebase（Google）：用于身份认证、数据库（Firestore）存储及推送通知基础设施。Firebase 遵循其隐私政策，详见 Firebase 隐私说明。
  • Apple：应用内购买通过 StoreKit 处理，交易由 Apple 处理。

我们不会将您的个人信息出售给第三方。

五、数据安全

我们采取合理措施保护您的数据，包括：

  • 使用 Firebase 提供的安全认证与加密传输
  • 密码以加密形式存储
  • 仅授权用户可访问自己的数据

六、您的权利

您有权：

  • 在设置中修改密码
  • 注销账户（需在设置中完成验证流程，注销后数据将被删除）
  • 通过退出登录清除本地登录状态

七、儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童。我们不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。

八、政策变更

我们可能不时更新本隐私政策。重大变更将在应用内或通过其他适当方式通知您。继续使用应用即表示您接受更新后的政策。

九、联系我们

如对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过应用内反馈或您已知的开发者联系方式与我们联系。

LUER Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

LUER is an iOS check-in app themed around "deer" (鹿), helping users record daily status and interact with friends. We value your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

To provide app functionality, we may collect:

  • Account information: Username, password, display name, 6-digit UID
  • Check-in data: Daily check-in records (Yes/No), consecutive days, endurance, energy
  • Social data: Friend list, friend requests, like records
  • Personal content: Date notes (remarks you voluntarily add)
  • Device & preferences: Theme, language, reminder time, login status (stored locally)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the above information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide check-in, friends, titles, and like features
  • Store your notes and preferences
  • Process in-app purchases (e.g., energy)
  • Send local check-in reminders (if enabled)

4. Data Storage & Third-Party Services

Your data is stored with trusted cloud providers:

  • Firebase (Google): Used for authentication, Firestore database, and push notification infrastructure. Firebase follows its own privacy policy; see Firebase Privacy.
  • Apple: In-app purchases are processed via StoreKit; transactions are handled by Apple.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:

  • Using Firebase secure authentication and encrypted transmission
  • Storing passwords in encrypted form
  • Restricting data access to authorized users only

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Change your password in Settings
  • Delete your account (via the verification flow in Settings; data will be removed upon deletion)
  • Clear local login status by signing out

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated in the app or through other appropriate means. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through in-app feedback or the developer contact you are aware of.
